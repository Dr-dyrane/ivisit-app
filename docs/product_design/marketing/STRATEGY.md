# Marketing Strategy: "The 5-Minute Frequency"

## I. Strategic Foundation

### The "Marketplace of Security"
iVisit is the infrastructure for on-demand medical security. We represent the shift from "Legacy Response" (Hope-based) to "Command Response" (Data-based).

### Brand Pillars
1. **The Tactical Frequency**: Operates on a 5-minute standard. Anything slower is legacy.
2. **Visual Certainty**: If you can't see it on the map, it doesn't exist.
3. **Quiet Authority**: Calm, direct communication. We don't scream for help; we provide command.

## II. Target Audience Segments

### 1. The On-Demand Native
- **Bio**: Users of Bolt, Uber, and Glovo. They value proximity over brand loyalty.
- **Hook**: "Care that moves as fast as your life."

### 2. The Institutional Overseer
- **Bio**: Hospital administrators and facility managers dealing with fragmented legacy dispatch.
- **Hook**: "Unified Command. Real-time capacity."

### 3. The Security-First Citizen
- **Bio**: Individuals prioritizing medical safety for their families and estates.
- **Hook**: "Certainty is the standard."

## III. Campaign Tone & Visual Language
- **Colors**: Deep Atmospheric Navy (`#0B0F1A`), Medical Red (`#86100E`).
- **Materials**: Liquid Glass, Subtle Blurs, Zero Borders.
- **Typography**: SF Pro Display (Bold tracking, heavy weights for authority).

## IV. Core Messaging Matrix

| Situation | Legacy Message | iVisit "Command" Message |
